Seaware Foundation wants to present you Podcast series, about the sunken ships. We are aware that everyone in the world is moving in a fast paste, it is not always possible to sit down and get information in written format, this is what lead us to create a series podcasts that will inform all about the ships and death zones, the current situation and what is happening. Seaware Foundation Podcast had two amazing young hosts, Sekgwele Christopher Ledwaba and Matevž Mencigar.

Toumas · Seaware Foundation podcast

Even though the casters are not from environmental background, both being engineering students, when they heard about the problems and stories, it became their big passion. Fresh look at situation at hand, from young adults, will be a perfect podcast for any age group, easy and understandable! Get inspired with them to make a change! Stay tune, for future episodes, guests and much more.
